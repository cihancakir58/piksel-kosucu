🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Küçük Dahi ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device ID, device type, operating system, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used, content accessed, and time spent in the App.
  • Crash Reports: We automatically collect crash data to improve app stability and performance.

2.2 Advertising Data

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App.
  • AdMob may collect information about your interactions with ads for advertising purposes.
  • For more information about AdMob's privacy practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

2.3 Analytics Data

  • We use Firebase Analytics to understand user behavior and improve our services.
  • This may include information about features you use, screen views, and user engagement metrics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To improve, personalize, and expand the App
  • To understand and analyze how you use the App
  • To develop new features and services
  • To display targeted advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Küçük Dahi is designed for children under 13. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • We have configured Google AdMob for child-directed treatment.
  • We do not display behaviorally targeted ads to children.
  • Parents or guardians can contact us to review, delete, or request we stop collection of personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For analytics and app stability monitoring
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK: For ad delivery and management

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to opt-out of certain data processing

9. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). If you have rights under these regulations, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.
